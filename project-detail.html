System Project 01 | January - April 2026

Visitor Management System

System Walkthrough

Complete interface screenshots of the PNP Visitor Management System.

A system for the Philippine National Police (PNP) under the Information Technology Management System. The PNP Visitor Management System digitizes appointment intake, identity capture, checkpoint verification, and visit tracking to reduce delays and improve operational consistency across facilities.

  • Helps personnel monitor appointment status and maintain accurate access records
  • Improves response times for regular and priority visits
  • Built to support secure, traceable, and consistent on-site visitor handling

During the internship, I also handled SQL debugging and database synchronization. I analyzed query structures and system logs to identify inconsistencies that caused delays or retrieval errors, then proposed fixes that improved reliability and accuracy.

  • Integrated the system with admin, office, gate, and lobby dashboards
  • Implemented role-based access control to protect sensitive information
  • Strengthened cybersecurity and compliance through permission-based access rules

Capstone Project 02 | August - December 2025

ENHANCING HEALTHCARE INSIGHTS THROUGH TIME SERIES FORECASTING FOR BARANGAY SAN JOSE PATAG DISEASE OUTBREAKS

System Walkthrough

Complete screenshots of the forecasting platform, data views, and workflow screens.

Primary care providers in barangay health stations play a critical front-line role in early disease detection and community monitoring. Yet many still rely on manual logbooks that limit retrieval, tracking, and timely analysis. This capstone addressed that gap with a digital forecasting platform for Barangay San Jose Patag.

  • Centralizes patient visit data for easier monitoring and faster access
  • Supports disease trend analysis and prediction using historical records
  • Helps health workers make informed decisions for community response planning

I played a major role as the main developer of Enhancing Healthcare Insights Through Time Series Forecasting for Barangay San Jose Patag Disease Outbreaks. I built the system using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, and integrated Python-based processing for time-series forecasting.

I also worked as a System Analyst and Data Analyst, gathering requirements, structuring the workflow, and preparing datasets so the SARIMA and Holt-Winters models could generate dependable predictions.

WST Final Project 03 | November - December 2024

PC Builder Shop Ordering System

System Walkthrough

Complete screenshots of customer flows, build setup, and admin-side workflows.

An online ordering system for a PC Builder Shop designed to deliver a complete e-commerce experience for customers while giving administrators efficient tools for product and order management.

  • Lets users browse products, explore featured items, and search with SQL-driven pagination
  • Includes a Build PC feature for assembling compatible custom computer setups
  • Supports secure authentication, cart management, checkout, and order history tracking

I served as the primary developer and handled both frontend and backend implementation using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. I also designed the system logic, database structure, and user flow to keep the platform responsive and reliable.

On the admin side, I developed product and inventory management, order monitoring, sales reporting, and customer communication features. The project demonstrates secure transactions, user-friendly navigation, and data-driven management in a modern shopping platform.
